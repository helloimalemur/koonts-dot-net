Originating from the Great Lakes area of Western New York. I was wildly interested at a young age in how computers, programs, and how malware worked. Many evenings were spent exploring vxheavens and other warez sites for code to play with.

Then came my linux Era. I fell in love with linux and FOSS immediately upon discovery, even after having to manually compile NVidia graphics and Broadcom wifi drivers.. Good ol' Ubuntu 5.10.

Soon thereafter, I was encompassed by the world of offensive security, my favorite being Web Application security and bug hunting.

Building things is just as satisfying as breaking them though! I've since taken up software development, my favorite language being Java.
